About the Application

One Plus Safety is the fastest and most secure women safety application, featuring SOS alert, fake call and GPS location tracking.

  • Emergency SOS notification to your beloved ones.
  • Fake Calls to deceive the wrongdoer and help evade danger.
  • Loud Siren to notify potential nearby people ready to help.
  • Automated Mail message feature to notify the nearest Police Station.

Need for the Application

  • Stasticially, India is one of the most unsafe countries for women.
  • Oneplus Safety will serve as the communication bridge between concerned authorities and the women in danger.
  • Integration of the police stations without physically being present.

Working of the application

  • Profile setup and input of concerned contact details.
  • Live GPS tracking and location sharing with nearest Police Station.
  • Notification sent to family and friends upon SOS and Siren activation.
